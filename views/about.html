What is "Stuck Wan Yah"?

"Stuck Wan Yah" is a pun for "stak wan yah or stak wan" [in tok pisin]. The term has subtle meanings. It is not known exactly when it was first coined up but was first used in 2016 single "Narawan", a collaboration by PNG's well-known artists 5-STAR, Jaro Local and Dr Doxx. In the song the initial meaning of the term refers to a person that has a major effect on your life or say that a girl, her crazyness, smile just won't fade away. It's stuck on your mind.
Its another meaning is used explicitly to refer to a person's looks. "Ol sa tok Hey stuck wan yeh"

The StuckWanYah website is a PNG revamp version of Facemash - a Howard version of hotornot.com website created by Mark Zuckerberg for rating Howard students based on their looks back in 2003. StuckWanYah works alongside Facebook. StuckWanYah depends on your Facebook data.
