What is "Stuck Wan Yah"?

"Stuck Wan Yah" is a pun for "stak wan yah", "stak wan" [in tok pisin]. The word has a subtle meanings. It was not known when it was first coined up but was first used by the PNG's well-known artists 5-STAR, Jaro Local and Dr Doxx in their 2016 collaboration single "Narawan". In that the word means you got addicted to your ex-boyfee or ex-girlfie and can't forget their crazynesses.
It also has another meaning which the term is used explicitly to refer to a person's looks.

"Hey Stuck Wan Yah!"

The StuckWanYah website is a PNG revamp version of Facemash - a Howard version of hotornot.com website created by Mark Zuckerberg for rating Howard students based on their looks back in 2003. The website works by making api calls to use Facebook Graph API to collect personal info and data.

Stuck Wan Yah app is been driven by a popular algorithm called "ELO" Rating System.
