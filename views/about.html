What is "Stuck Wan Yah"?

Stuck Wan Yah [Stak Wan Yah] or "Stak Wan" is a pun for telling somebody off base on their looks

Stuck Wan Yah is a PNG revamp version of Facemash - a website created
by Mark Zuckerberg for rating Howard students based on their looks back in 2003.

Stuck Wan Yah app is been driven by a popular algorithm called "ELO" Rating System.
